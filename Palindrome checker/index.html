Whats a palindrome?

A palindrome is a word, sentence, verse, or even number that reads the same backward or forward.

We use palindromes everyday without thinking about it. Common palindromic—that’s the adjective for palindrome—words include: noon, civic, racecar, level, and mom.The Finnish word for “soapstone vendor” is supposedly the longest palindrome in everyday use: saippuakivikauppias.
